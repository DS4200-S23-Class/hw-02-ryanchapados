Task Abstraction

  • High Level - Present. This tool is clearly trying to show that in the last 3 days the temperature has been abnormally high, and since the conclusion is already known it is being presented.

  • Mid Level - Locate. As the goal of this tool is displaying the temperature in the last 3 days in relation to everything else, the location of the temperature in the visual is the only unknown.

  • Low Level - Compare. The visual is trying to show that the last 3 days were higher than average, which is comparing the last 3 days to everything else.

  • Target - Outliers. The last 3 days is being compared to all the other data to show how much of an outlier the last 3 days are.

  • Mid Level - Lookup. Both the target and the location are known in this scenario.

  • Low Level - Compare. The visual is showing the comparison between the two cities, to show that the trends in temperature are similar.
